What you get

Everything a library needs,
nothing it doesn't.

Built around the workflows librarians and readers actually use — no bloat, no confusion.

Instant Borrowing

Check availability in real time and borrow in a single click. Your due date is set automatically.

Smart Reservations

Book unavailable? Join the FIFO queue. When it's returned, it's automatically assigned to you.

Fine Management

Late returns are handled automatically. View your fine history and pay directly from your dashboard.

Reading Sessions

Track time spent reading in the library without borrowing. Check in and out with one tap.

Librarian Dashboard

Full admin control — manage books, members, loans, and overdue records from a single panel.

Member Profiles

Every reader gets a profile with avatar, borrow history, reservations, and fine records in one place.
